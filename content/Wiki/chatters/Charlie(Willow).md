# Charlie(Willow)

> *(for once we know the origin of the mind, we cannot doubt that brute animals are sentient)* —Spinoza

## About

Charlie(Willow), also known as **seinzumtode** or simply **Charlie**, is a philosophy community member since 2019 and the self-described second-in-command Deleuzian to the all-holy Willow. Charlie is Willow. Well, Willow is really Willow, but Charlie is Willow. Willow is ineffable.

Based in the United States, Charlie holds a B.S. in Philosophy and is looking to pursue an advanced degree.

## Philosophical Profile

### Influences

- **Gilles Deleuze** — Primary allegiance; defender of the philosophy of difference
- **G.W.F. Hegel** — Dialectics, Absolute Idealism
- **Immanuel Kant** — Critical Philosophy
- **Baruch Spinoza** — Monism, Ethics
- **Salomon Maimon** — Post-Kantian rationalism

### Top 5 Works

1. *Difference and Repetition* — Gilles Deleuze
2. *Critique of Pure Reason* — Immanuel Kant
3. *Phenomenology of Spirit* — G.W.F. Hegel
4. *Tractatus Logico-Philosophicus* — Ludwig Wittgenstein
5. *Ethics* — Baruch Spinoza

### Known Positions

Charlie regularly defends Deleuzian critiques of identitarian philosophy — the primacy of difference over identity, and the problems with subordinating difference to representation.
